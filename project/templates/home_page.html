Parking Spot System Overview

The Parking Spot System is designed to simplify the search for available parking spaces, especially in crowded areas. It uses advanced geolocation technologies to direct users accurately to the nearest available parking spot based on their current location.

The system leverages the Genetic Algorithm for Resource Allocation, a powerful artificial intelligence technique that mimics the process of natural selection. This algorithm optimizes the distribution of parking spots to minimize the distance between users and the nearest available spot.

By addressing common issues associated with urban congestion, the system enhances user experience and reduces traffic pressure. It ensures better resource utilization and provides convenience for users at every step.
